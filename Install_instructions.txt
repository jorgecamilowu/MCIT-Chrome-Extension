Thank you for your interest in using MCIT Portal!

Please follow the above instructions to install the MCIT Portal extension:
	1. Open chrome and go to the following link: chrome://extensions/
	2. On the upper right corner, you will see a toggle for Developer mode. Go
	   ahead and enable that.
	3. You will then see a button that says 'Load unpacked' click that button and
	   choose the extension folder.
	4. Enjoy!
